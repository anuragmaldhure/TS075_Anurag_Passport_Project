Frequently Asked Questions
Question 1: How do I apply for a passport?

Answer: You can apply for a passport by clicking on the 'Apply for Fresh Passport' button on the homepage.

Question 2: How do I check my application status?

Answer: You can check your application status by clicking on the 'Check your passport application status' button on the homepage.

Question 3: What are the fees for passport applications?

Answer: The fees for passport applications can be found by clicking on the 'Make payment for Application Fees' button on the homepage.
